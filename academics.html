Curricular

Academic activities are designed following the guidelines enlisted by CBSE to give a holistic understanding of the Core Subjects through constructive pedagogical practices. In addition to it, we at SKVV give special focus to NEET, IIT-JEE programs giving wings to the dreams and aspirations of the budding Doctors and Engineers. NEET and IIT-JEE classes are conducted by experienced NEET and IIT-JEE faculty members who are having a wide range of experience in guiding our SKVVians to give color to their dreams. Special coaching classes are conducted for appearing all the competitive examinations like NTSE, SOF, etc.,

KinderGarten

During this period of rapid growth, the students are placed in a secure, supportive and stimulating environment that enables them to develop the skills required for successful participation in school life.

The child needs to develop self-esteem and self-assurance will be fulfilled while the Kindergarten provides an atmosphere where he feels understood and accepted. This kind of atmosphere creates a basis for safety, where the child gains experience in the world and can build relationships.

Both preschool and Faculties are seen as providing the steppingstones for social and academic success. Kindergarten students have a program that uses scaffolding to present concepts and ideas and guided play. These activities are appropriate and can be used in the classroom and by parents at home. We at SKVV believe that the majority of these activities can be carried out in the school premises, reinforcing the fact that social and academic success among the young minds as a team effort.

Primary Level

Students from classes 1 to 5 continue their journey through the primary years with the guidance of homeroom and specialist teachers. Participation is compulsory in all extra-curricular activities to ensure that academics are balanced with a variety of leisure pursuits, special days and celebrations.

The primary stage is very crucial stage in the life of the child. So, the objectives of primary education are different from those in the middle stage of education. At the primary level   child’s curiosity, creativity and activity in general should not be restricted by rigid and unattractive methods of teaching and learning. We at SKVV provide compassionate environment for both physical and mental development.

Upper Primary Level

Upper primary education means educating all students up to the age of 14, which is equivalent to completing the second cycle of primary education. This involves facilitating easy geographic access to enroll all eligible students in the upper primary schools, providing a reasonable level of facilities and infrastructure in these primary schools.

This programme has a board-based curriculum frame work which encourages students to question, evaluate and to think critically and independently. SKVV falls in line with the latest educational trends and practices adopted in the NCF 2005 to ensure a holistic development of the students. We at SKVV provide compassionate environment for overall development in a child.

Secondary Level

Secondary education is in most countries the phase in the education continuum responsible for the development of the young during their adolescence, the most rapid phase of their physical, mental and emotional growth.  The Secondary School Curriculum acknowledges the fact that subjects like Languages, Mathematics, Sciences and Social Science help the cognitive development of the child and therefore, require a greater academic emphasis. Further, CBSE also envisions the all-round development of students in consonance with the holistic approach to education and therefore, emphasizes integration of co-curricular domains with curricular activities in an equitable manner.

We at SKVV provide a challenging environment for the all round development. The students are being sharpened by experienced facilitators and prepared the secondary students to the senior secondary level education.

Senior Secondary Level

The Senior Secondary Program provides a strong foundation for understanding of the CORE Subjects to make learning easier in top Universities. It is purely based on NCERT and CBSE curriculum to mould and prepare the students to compete the challenges in the modern world.

The Program mainly incorporates the 21st century necessities like Medicine (NEET, AIIMS etc.,), Engineering (IITs, NITs), Chartered Accountancy (CA). It gives the sound academic and organizational skills to make the students understand the global issues. It gives ample opportunities to respect others and appreciate the similarities and differences, as a whole it’s a path for Academic Excellence.
